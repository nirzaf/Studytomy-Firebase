Home School

Homeschooling's definition varies among individuals, but it broadly refers to the education of a child outside the conventional school system by a parent, guardian, or tutor. A student can be homeschooled with either a physical or online tutor. This occurs on a full-time basis, as all children are legally required to receive a full-time education from the age of five. The legal age for full-time education may vary from country to country.

There is no one-size-fits-all approach to homeschooling. The philosophies, educational structures, and curriculum are decided by the parent and child, with the child's growth and progress being the primary focus.

Homeschooling can be as similar or different to the traditional school system as desired, as long as it ensures that the child receives a full-time education tailored to their age and ability.
