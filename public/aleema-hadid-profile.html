Mrs. Aleema Hadid - Experienced Educator

Mrs. Aleema Hadid is a passionate and experienced educator with over 10 years of experience teaching English and Psychology at the secondary and college levels. She is committed to providing her students with a rigorous and engaging learning experience that prepares them for success in their future academic and professional endeavors.


Teaching Philosophy

Mrs. Hadid's teaching philosophy is student-centered and focused on developing critical thinking skills, communication skills, and a love of learning. She believes that all students can succeed, and she works hard to create a supportive and inclusive classroom environment where all students feel valued and respected.


Teaching Methods

In her previous role as a high school English teacher, Mrs. Hadid developed and implemented a variety of innovative teaching methods, including project-based learning, peer tutoring, and differentiated instruction. She also worked closely with students to develop their writing skills and prepare them for college-level coursework.


Current Role

Mrs. Hadid is currently a college English teacher at a local community college. In this role, she teaches a variety of courses, including composition, literature, and critical thinking. She is known for her engaging lectures, her ability to create a positive learning environment, and her commitment to helping her students succeed.


Accomplishments

  • Developed and implemented a new English language arts curriculum for middle school students that was aligned with the Common Core State Standards.
  • Led a team of teachers in developing a new peer tutoring program that has helped to improve student achievement in English language arts.
  • Mentored several new teachers and helped them to develop their teaching skills.
  • Received the "Teacher of the Year" award by her school district on two occasions.
